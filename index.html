FAQ

Quick answers for families.

Do you have openings?

Yes. There are currently 2 full-time spots open for ages 2-12, with a 3rd full-time spot expected when the school year starts in August 2026. Availability can change, so please message with your child's age, schedule, and hoped-for start date.

What ages do you serve?

Stacey's Little Stars currently serves children ages 2-12, from toddlers through school-age children.

What are the hours?

Care is generally available Monday through Friday from 7am-5pm for full-time families.

What is the rate?

The current full-time rate is $53/day, with meals and snacks included.

Do you offer part-time care?

Openings are currently full-time only. Keeping a consistent weekly group helps children settle into the routine and helps families know what to expect.

Are meals included?

Yes. Meals and snacks are included as part of the daily routine.

Do you accept Community Connection?

Yes. Families using Community Connection are welcome. Please mention it in your first message so Stacey can confirm the care schedule, start-date timing, and any paperwork details before enrollment.

Do you provide school pickup or dropoff?

Stacey's Little Stars does not provide school pickup or dropoff by car. Bus support may be available for Veterans Elementary and Norris Middle School because nearby bus stops are close to the home.

How do I inquire?

Message on Instagram with your child's age, the schedule you need, your hoped-for start date, and whether you plan to use Community Connection. That gives Stacey the details needed to reply with the best next step.
